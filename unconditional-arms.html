Kinship was written by Jeff Wright (Unconditional Arms) for the birth of his son Owen, and can be compared to other acts as Godspeed! You Black Emperor and The Appleseed Cast.

"New life is a beautiful thing, something that one is eager to share with the world … Love overflows on this release, especially in the dialogue of 'First Look', but it doesn’t overwhelm … 'The Family Tree' possesses a similar sense of ebullience. The peaceful finale, 'Rest', implies something that new parents don’t encounter often; but in this case, if Jeffrey can get it, his rest will have been well-deserved." – a closer listen
