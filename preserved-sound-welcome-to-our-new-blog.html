Preserved Sound – Welcome To Our New Blog!
09/19/2016 Comments

Welcome to our new blog!

Hello. Welcome to our new blog. We'll be posting a range of articles, including interviews with our artists, reviews of our releases and general Preserved Sound news. If you'd like to contribute to this blog, please email us at: hello@preservedsound.com

This inaugural blog is just to tell you that we've moved! After five years ago in Krakow, Poland, we've just moved to Hebden Bridge in the north of England! The reasons for this are many, but we're really excited to be able to continue releasing music from this vibrant market town in West Yorkshire!

​This also means that we'll be changing our postage costs. But the good news is that after a busy summer moving and settling in, we have some wonderful new music coming your way!

September sees the re-release of Footfalls Echo by Visionary Hours. Originally released by Hibernate in 2015, we've had loads of requests to re-release the album! So here it is! Listen to and/or preorder Footfalls Echo below.

​In October, Italian duo Covarino/Incorvaia make their debut on Preserved Sound with their live an unedited, instrumental ambient rock album Perugia. Watch a preview of the duo recording the album in studio below.​

And in November, Russian composer Max Ananyev releases his new album Communication, combining classical guitar and electroacoustic ambience. Watch Max playing "Oak Trees" – the stunning final track on the album – below.

Look out for the preorders of these fantastic albums, or sign up to our mailing list for advance warning!

We're also waiting on new albums from Adrian Lane, Aaron Martin and Poppy Nogood ... more news about that coming soon! In the meantime, do check out our latest releases from Tess Said So, Poppy Nogood and Ales Tsurko!

Finally, thanks for all the support you've shown us over the last five years. We really do appreciate all your comments and emails!
